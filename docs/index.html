Installation

1

Download and unzip

Download the zip file above and double-click to extract it.

2

Move to Applications

Drag CapslockMute.app to your Applications folder.

3

Grant Accessibility permission

On first launch, macOS will ask for Accessibility permission. This is required to detect your Caps Lock key.

Requirements

  • macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later
  • Tandem app installed
  • No Karabiner-Elements (conflicts with key remapping)

Privacy

Your data stays on your Mac

CapslockMute doesn't collect any data. It doesn't connect to the internet. It just listens for Caps Lock and sends a mute shortcut to Tandem. That's it.
